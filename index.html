Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Unemployed...For Now! ("we," "our," or "the app") is a mobile application intended solely for adults aged 18 and older located in the United States. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Account Information (provided by you):

  • Name and email address — collected when you create an account through our sign-up process.
  • Onboarding preferences — information you provide during setup to personalize your experience (such as your state of employment and personal goals). This information is stored with your account and is not shared externally.

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Usage data — anonymous app usage patterns, session frequency, screen views, and feature engagement.
  • Device information — device model, operating system version, app version, and general performance metrics.
  • Crash reports — technical diagnostic data collected when the app encounters an error, used solely to identify and fix bugs.

Information We Do NOT Collect:

  • We do not collect or access your device location. Any state-specific information in the app is based on your manual selection during onboarding.
  • We do not collect financial information, Social Security numbers, or other sensitive personal identifiers.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, camera, or microphone.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing the service — to create and manage your account, personalize your experience, and deliver relevant content based on your preferences.
  • Improving the app — to understand how features are used, identify bugs, and make improvements.
  • Communication — to respond to support requests or notify you of important changes to the app or this policy.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the app. These services may collect and process data on our behalf in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase Authentication — for secure account creation and sign-in.
  • Google Cloud Firestore — for storing your account data and app preferences.
  • Google Firebase Analytics — for anonymous usage analytics to help us understand app performance and feature engagement.
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics — for collecting crash reports and diagnostic data to improve app stability.
  • Google AdMob — our app includes advertising infrastructure. When ads are enabled, AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can learn more about Google's advertising practices at Google Privacy Policy.

These services are provided by Google LLC. You can review Google's privacy practices at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Advertising

Our app may display advertisements through Google AdMob. When ads are shown, AdMob may use device advertising identifiers to serve ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • Android: Settings > Privacy > Ads

We do not use your name, email address, or onboarding data for advertising or ad targeting purposes.

6. Data Sharing & Selling

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

We do not use your name or email address for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes. We may share data only with the trusted service providers listed in Section 4 above, solely for the purpose of operating and improving the app, and under strict confidentiality obligations.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Your data is transmitted using industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) and stored on secure servers provided by Google Firebase. While no system is 100% secure, we are committed to safeguarding your information to the best of our ability.

8. Data Retention & Account Deletion

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time using the in-app account deletion option found in your account settings. When you delete your account:

  • Your personal information (name, email, and onboarding data) will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
  • Anonymous, aggregated usage data that cannot be linked back to you may be retained for analytics purposes.

You may also contact us at the email below to request data access, correction, or deletion.

9. Children's Privacy

Unemployed...For Now! is not intended for anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a person under 18, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct any inaccurate personal data.
  • Delete your account and associated personal data (via in-app deletion or by contacting us).
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

To exercise any of these rights, you can use the in-app settings or contact us at the email address below.

11. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to request deletion, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. As stated above, we do not sell personal information. To exercise your CCPA rights, please contact us at the email address below.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. If we make material changes, we will notify you by updating the effective date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, providing notice within the app. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
