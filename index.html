Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Unemployed...For Now is intended solely for adults aged 18 and older located in the United States. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency about how we collect and use your data.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name and email address (collected when you create an account).
  • Authentication Data: If you sign in via Apple or Google, we receive your Apple ID or Google account information (name, email, and profile data) needed to authenticate you. We do not store your password.
  • App Usage & Analytics: Anonymous app usage, sessions, features accessed, and performance metrics collected through Firebase Analytics to help us improve the app.
  • Mood & Wellness Check-ins: When you complete daily mood check-ins (1–5 scale ratings), this data is stored in our database and used only to provide personalized guidance within the app.
  • Device Information: We collect limited device identifiers and OS version through Firebase to track crashes, stability, and app performance.

3. How We Use Information

We use this data only to:

  • Provide and maintain the app service
  • Authenticate and manage your account
  • Deliver personalized task recommendations and motivation
  • Improve app stability, performance, and features
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Service Providers

We use the following third-party services that may collect and process data:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore Database, Remote Config, Analytics, Crash Reporting): Firebase handles account authentication, stores your account data and mood check-ins, manages app configuration, and collects usage analytics and crash reports. Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Apple Sign-In: If you sign in with Apple, Apple's privacy policy applies: https://www.apple.com/privacy/
  • Google Sign-In: If you sign in with Google, Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Sharing & Sale

We do not sell any data to third parties.
We do not use your name or email for advertising or profiling.
We may share data only with trusted service providers (e.g., Firebase for cloud hosting and analytics) under strict confidentiality agreements.

Note on Advertising: Ad infrastructure may be integrated in future updates. When enabled, advertising SDKs may collect device identifiers and IP addresses for ad serving. Users will be notified via privacy policy updates before this occurs.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Your Rights & Data Retention

Data Access & Deletion: You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at unemployedfornow1@gmail.com.

Data Retention: We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law. Analytics data (usage metrics) may be retained for up to 14 months for app improvement purposes.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA): If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, including the right to know what data we collect, the right to delete, and the right to opt-out of any future data sales (though we do not currently sell data).
